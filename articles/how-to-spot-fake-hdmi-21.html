How to Identify Counterfeit HDMI 2.1 Cables

Verification | Updated April 2026

The Risk of "Fake" Cables

Many cables claim "8K" or "HDMI 2.1" compatibility on the box but fail to deliver the 48Gbps bandwidth required for the PS5. This leads to screen flickering, blackouts, or a forced downgrade to 60Hz. Here is how you verify your hardware.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Scan the Hologram: Download the official "HDMI Cable Certification" app on your smartphone.
  2. Locate the Sticker: Find the "Ultra High Speed HDMI" sticker on the cable's packaging. It must have a unique holographic code.
  3. Verify the Brand: Use the app to scan the code. It should return the exact brand and model name of the cable you purchased.
  4. Physical Inspection: Look for the "Ultra High Speed" text printed directly on the cable jacket itself. Genuine cables rarely leave this blank.
