How to Spot Fake HDMI 2.1 Cables

Tutorial | April 2026

Look for the QR Code

Genuine Ultra High Speed HDMI cables come with a holographic sticker and a QR code on the box.

The Certification App

  1. Download the HDMI Cable Certification app on your phone.
  2. Scan the QR code on the cable packaging.
  3. The app will tell you immediately if the cable is officially certified for 48Gbps.
