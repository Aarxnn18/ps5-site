Best SSD for PS5 (2026 Guide)

With modern games often exceeding 100GB, the PS5's internal storage fills up fast. Upgrading with a compatible M.2 SSD is the best way to keep your entire library ready to play without sacrificing speed.

2026 Pro Tip: Always ensure your SSD has a heatsink. High-speed Gen4 drives generate significant heat that can cause performance drops if not cooled properly.

PS5 SSD Requirements

To be compatible with the PlayStation 5, an SSD must meet these specific technical standards:

  • Interface: PCIe Gen4 x4 M.2 NVMe SSD
  • Capacity: 250GB – 8TB
  • Read Speed: 5,500MB/s or faster recommended
  • Form Factor: 2230, 2242, 2260, 2280, or 22110
  • Heatsink: Required (either built-in or added manually)

Common Questions (FAQ)

Does a faster SSD make games load faster?

Yes, but only up to a certain point. While any drive over 5,500MB/s works, drives reaching 7,000MB/s+ ensure you are ready for future "DirectStorage" style technologies in newer games.

Can I use a PCIe Gen3 SSD?

No. The PS5 requires the higher bandwidth of Gen4. A Gen3 drive will not be recognized by the console for game storage.
