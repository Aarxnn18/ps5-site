Best PS5 SSDs: 2026 Performance Analysis

Hardware Analysis | Updated April 2026

The Need for Speed

In 2026, games are pushing the 150GB mark. To run these titles natively, your expansion drive must meet strict PCIe Gen4 requirements. We've tested the latest NVMe drives to ensure they hit the mandatory 5,500MB/s read speeds.

Pro Tip: Always ensure your chosen SSD has a built-in heatsink. The PS5 internal bay has limited airflow, and high-speed Gen4 drives can throttle performance if they get too hot.

Top Rated Drives

After extensive benchmarking, the following drives represent the gold standard for PS5 storage expansion: The WD_Black SN850X and the Samsung 990 Pro.
