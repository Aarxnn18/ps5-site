Best SSD for PS5 (2026 Guide)

Find the best SSD upgrades for faster loading times and more storage.

Why Upgrade Your PS5 SSD?

The PS5 comes with limited storage. Upgrading your SSD allows you to install more games and improve loading speeds.

Recommended SSD Types

  • Budget NVMe SSD (good performance)
  • Mid-range NVMe SSD (best value)
  • High-end NVMe SSD (maximum speed)

What to Look For

Make sure the SSD supports PCIe Gen4 and has high read speeds for PS5 compatibility.

Conclusion

Any compatible NVMe SSD will significantly improve your PS5 storage experience.
