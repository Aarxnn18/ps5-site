Expert Advice: What We Look For

In 2026, a PS5 SSD isn't just about storage—it's about bandwidth. We specifically look for drives that exceed the 5,500MB/s Sony requirement to ensure that future-gen "DirectStorage" titles load instantly without stuttering.
