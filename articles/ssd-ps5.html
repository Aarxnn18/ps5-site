Best SSD for PS5 (2026 Guide)

Upgrading your PS5 SSD gives you more storage space for modern games while maintaining fast load times and smooth performance.

Quick Recommendation: A PCIe Gen4 NVMe SSD with a heatsink offers the best balance of speed and compatibility for PS5.

What Makes a Good PS5 SSD?

The best PS5 SSDs combine high read speeds, reliable cooling, and strong storage capacity. Sony recommends Gen4 NVMe drives with fast sequential read performance.

Important Features

  • High-speed Gen4 NVMe performance
  • Built-in heatsink support
  • Reliable long-term durability
  • Strong game loading performance

Storage Recommendations

1TB is ideal for most players, while 2TB or larger works better for large digital libraries.
