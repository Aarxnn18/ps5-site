Best OLED TVs for PS5 Pro: 2026 Edition

Display Hardware | Updated May 2026

Expert Advice: Why OLED for PS5 Pro?

To truly see the benefits of PSSR (PlayStation Spectral Super Resolution) and advanced Ray Tracing on the PS5 Pro, a standard LED display is no longer sufficient. You need the "Infinite Contrast" and sub-1ms response times only OLED can provide. These 2026 models feature the latest HDMI 2.1a specifications, supporting up to 144Hz VRR for ultra-smooth high-frame-rate performance.

1. LG OLED Evo AI G6 (55-inch) - The Flagship Choice

The G6 is the pinnacle of 2026 display tech. Featuring Micro Lens Array (MLA+) technology, it achieves HDR brightness levels that rival high-end LED panels while maintaining perfect blacks. Its Alpha 11 AI processor is purpose-built to handle the PS5 Pro's output, ensuring every pixel of upscaled detail is razor-sharp.

2. Samsung S95F QD-OLED (55-inch) - Vibrant Precision

The S95F remains the gold standard for color volume. By combining Quantum Dots with OLED, it produces reds and greens that standard OLEDs cannot reach. Its "Glare-Free" coating is a must-have for gamers who play in brighter living rooms without sacrificing the deep blacks OLED is known for.

Check Samsung S95F Price

3. Sony Bravia 8 OLED (55-inch) - The Eco-System King

The "Perfect for PlayStation" badge isn't just marketing. The Bravia 8 features Auto HDR Tone Mapping and Auto Genre Picture Mode, which calibrate the TV specifically for the PS5 Pro's hardware. While it stays at 120Hz, its motion handling and cinematic processing are unmatched for story-driven exclusives.

Check Bravia 8 Price

4. Samsung S90F QD-OLED (55-inch) - The Performance Pick

If you want the speed of the flagship S95F without the premium price tag, the S90F is the smart play. It retains the lightning-fast 144Hz refresh rate and punchy QD-OLED panel, making it a favorite for competitive shooters like Call of Duty where motion clarity is life or death.

Check Samsung S90F Price

5. LG OLED Evo AI C6 (65-inch) - Best Value Large Screen

The C-series continues to be the world's most popular gaming TV for a reason. The C6 offers four full-bandwidth HDMI 2.1 ports, allowing you to connect a PS5 Pro, a PC, and a soundbar without compromises. For a 65-inch setup, it offers the best balance of size, speed, and price.
