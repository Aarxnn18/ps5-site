Best OLED TVs for PS5 Pro: 2026 Edition

Display Hardware | Updated April 2026

The Peak of Visual Fidelity

For the PS5 Pro, a standard LED display is no longer sufficient. To truly see the benefits of PSSR (AI Upscaling) and advanced Ray Tracing, you need a panel with "Infinite Contrast" and a sub-1ms response time. These 2026 models feature the latest HDMI 2.1a specifications and 144Hz refresh rates.

Top 5 OLED Gaming Picks

Model Panel Tech Max Refresh
LG G5 OLED Evo MLA+ OLED 144Hz / VRR
Samsung S95F QD-OLED (Gen 4) 144Hz / Glare-Free
Sony Bravia 9 II XR OLED Contrast 120Hz / Auto HDR
Panasonic Z95B Master OLED Pro 144Hz / Atmos Built-in
LG C5 Series OLED Evo 144Hz / Best Value

Why These Models?

The LG G5 remains the king of gaming due to its four full-bandwidth HDMI 2.1 ports and ultra-low input lag. However, the Samsung S95F offers the most vibrant colors in the QD-OLED market, making it the better choice for HDR-heavy titles.
