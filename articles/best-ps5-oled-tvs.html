Best OLED TVs for PS5 Pro: 2026 Edition

Display Hardware | Updated May 2026

Expert Advice: Why OLED for PS5 Pro?

To truly see the benefits of PSSR (PlayStation Spectral Super Resolution) and advanced Ray Tracing on the PS5 Pro, a standard LED display is no longer sufficient. You need the "Infinite Contrast" and sub-1ms response times only OLED can provide. Our 2026 selections focus on panels like the **QD-OLED S95D** and **BRAVIA A95L**, which offer the high-bandwidth 48Gbps throughput required for 4K/120Hz gaming without chroma subsampling.

1. Sony BRAVIA XR A95L - The Master Series Choice

The A95L remains a titan of color accuracy and peak brightness. Using Sony’s Cognitive Processor XR, it leverages the PS5 Pro's hardware to provide Auto HDR Tone Mapping. For gamers who prioritize the most realistic, cinematic image possible in 2026, the QD-OLED A95L is the definitive "Perfect for PlayStation" display.

2. Samsung S95D QD-OLED - Vibrant & Glare-Free

The S95D is the choice for performance enthusiasts. It features a revolutionary "OLED Glare-Free" coating that allows for daytime gaming without the distracting reflections typical of glossy OLEDs. With a 144Hz refresh rate and punchy Quantum Dot colors, it is the ideal companion for high-speed PS5 Pro titles.

3. LG C6 OLED Evo (2026 Model) - The Gaming Standard

The 2026 C6 model continues LG's dominance in the gaming space. It offers four full-bandwidth HDMI 2.1 ports and the most robust VRR (Variable Refresh Rate) support on the market. For the gamer who wants a high-refresh-rate 144Hz experience on a 65-inch screen without the flagship price tag, the C6 is unbeatable.

4. LG OLED Evo AI G6 - The Brightest Star

Featuring Micro Lens Array (MLA+) technology, the G6 achieves HDR highlights that rival LED panels while maintaining perfect ink-black levels. If your setup is in a bright room and you don't want a matte finish, the G6's massive brightness headroom is the solution.

5. Sony Bravia 8 OLED - The Balanced Contender

The Bravia 8 is the smart pick for users who use their PS5 Pro as a media hub. It provides exceptional upscaling for non-4K content and features the same seamless PS5 integration as the flagship A95L, ensuring your console settings are always optimized.
