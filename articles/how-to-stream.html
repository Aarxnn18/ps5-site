How to Stream Directly from Your PS5

Network Tip: For a stable stream, use a LAN cable. Wi-Fi can cause "dropped frames" which makes your video lag for viewers.

Step 1: Link Your Accounts

Go to Settings > Users and Accounts > Link with Other Services. Choose Twitch or YouTube and follow the QR code instructions on your phone.

Step 2: Connect Your Camera & Mic

Plug in your USB camera or headset mic. Go to Settings > Sound > Microphone to ensure your voice is being picked up clearly.

Step 3: Go Live!

While in a game, press the Create Button on your controller and select Broadcast. Give your stream a title and hit "Go Live."

Step 4: Managing Chat

You can display your chat on the side of your screen or use a smartphone to read comments so you can interact with your viewers in real-time.
