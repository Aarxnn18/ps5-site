How to Stream Directly From PS5 to Twitch & YouTube

Streaming Guide | Updated April 2026

The Creator Dashboard

The PS5 features a built-in capture card capable of streaming in 1080p 60fps. While it doesn't offer the complexity of a PC, it is the most efficient way to start your streaming career with zero extra cost.

Pro Creator Tip: Internal streaming relies heavily on your upload speed. For a jitter-free 1080p broadcast, avoid Wi-Fi. A high-bandwidth Ethernet connection is mandatory for professional stability. See our Connection Guide for network-ready cabling.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Link Accounts: Go to Settings > Users and Accounts > Linked Services and sign in to Twitch or YouTube.
  2. Open Broadcast Menu: While in a game, press the Create Button (to the left of the touchpad) and select the Broadcast icon.
  3. Configure Video: Select "Broadcast Options" to toggle your PS5 Camera and Chat Overlay. If you find the default camera lighting poor, check our Streaming Gear guide for ring light recommendations.
  4. Audio Check: Ensure your microphone is clear. For 2026 broadcasts, internal controller mics are often too noisy. We recommend a dedicated headset from our Best Microphones list.
  5. Go Live: Enter your stream title and hit Go Live. Your light bar will turn orange to indicate you are on air.
