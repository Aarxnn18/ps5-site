How to Stream Directly From PS5 to Twitch & YouTube

Streaming Guide | Updated April 2026

The Creator Dashboard

The PS5 features a built-in capture card capable of streaming in 1080p 60fps. While it doesn't offer the complexity of a PC, it is the most efficient way to start your streaming career with zero extra cost.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Link Accounts: Go to Settings > Users and Accounts > Linked Services and sign in to Twitch or YouTube.
  2. Open Broadcast Menu: While in a game, press the Create Button and select the Broadcast icon.
  3. Configure Video: Select "Broadcast Options" to toggle your PS5 Camera and Chat Overlay visibility.
  4. Go Live: Enter your stream title and hit Go Live. Your light bar will turn orange to indicate you are on air.
