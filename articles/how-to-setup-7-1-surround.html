Configuring 7.1.4 Surround Sound on PS5

Audio Calibration | Updated April 2026

Understanding the Digital Path

To achieve true 7.1 or Atmos audio, your signal chain must be perfect. If you connect your PS5 to your TV and the TV to your soundbar, you must use the **eARC** (Enhanced Audio Return Channel) port. Standard ARC does not have the bandwidth for uncompressed 7.1 or Atmos bitstreams.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Physical eARC Check: Ensure your HDMI 2.1 cable is plugged into the HDMI port labeled eARC on your TV.
  2. Format Selection: Go to Settings > Sound > Audio Output. Set "Audio Format (Priority)" to Dolby Atmos.
  3. Device Type: In the same menu, set "Output Device" to HDMI Device (AV Amplifier).
  4. Channel Count: Ensure "Number of Channels" is set to 7.1 ch or matching your specific amplifier capability.
  5. Speaker Alignment: Select Adjust Speaker Positions. Use the on-screen diagram to virtually move the speakers to match where they actually sit in your room.
