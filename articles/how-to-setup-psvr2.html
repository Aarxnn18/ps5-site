PSVR2 Expert Setup & Room Calibration

VR Setup | Updated April 2026

Optimizing the VR Experience

Setup is more than just plugging in a USB-C cable. To get the clearest image and prevent motion sickness, you must calibrate the eye-tracking and lens distance specifically to your face.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Physical Connection: Plug the PSVR2 cable into the front USB-C port of the PS5.
  2. Lens Adjustment: Use the dial on the top left of the headset to move the lenses until the on-screen text becomes perfectly sharp.
  3. Eye Tracking: Navigate to Settings > Accessories > PSVR2 > Eye Tracking and follow the red dot to calibrate.
  4. Room Scanning: Look around your room while wearing the headset to let the cameras map your furniture and floor for a safe "Play Area."
