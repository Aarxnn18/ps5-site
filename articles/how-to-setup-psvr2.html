PSVR2 First-Time Setup

Tutorial | April 2026

Safety Note: Clear a 2m x 2m area for Roomscale mode to avoid bumping into furniture.

The Connection

Plug the single USB-C cable into the front of your PS5. Power on the headset and follow the on-screen lens adjustment steps.

Eye Tracking

Make sure to calibrate eye-tracking in the settings for the best visual clarity and performance.
