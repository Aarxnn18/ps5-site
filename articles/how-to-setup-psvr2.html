How to Setup Your PSVR2

Safety First: Ensure you have a clear play area of at least 2m x 2m for room-scale gaming.

Step 1: The One-Cable Connection

Plug the USB-C cable from the headset directly into the front port of your PS5. Press the power button on the bottom of the headset scope.

Step 2: Pair the Sense Controllers

Connect each controller to the console using a USB cable and press the PS Button to sync them. You only need to do this once.

Step 3: Calibrate Eye-Tracking

Follow the on-screen red dot with your eyes. This allows the PS5 to focus detail exactly where you are looking (Foveated Rendering), which makes games look much sharper.

Step 4: Set Your Play Area

The PSVR2 will "scan" your room using its cameras. Use the controllers to "paint" a floor boundary to ensure you don't bump into furniture.
