The Definitive PS5 Controller Review

Input Devices | Updated April 2026

Expert Advice: Precision & Durability

In 2026, the biggest leap in controller tech is the move away from traditional potentiometers to **Hall Effect sensors**. These use magnets to track movement, virtually eliminating stick drift—the #1 killer of controllers.
