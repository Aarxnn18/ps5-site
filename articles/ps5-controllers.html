Best PS5 Controllers (2026 Guide)

Whether you are looking for more immersion or a competitive edge, choosing the right controller is the biggest upgrade you can make to your PS5 setup.

Pro Tip: Look for controllers with Hall Effect sensors if you want to avoid "stick drift" permanently.

New Guide: Having connection issues? Read our PS5 Controller Pairing & Reset Guide.

Top Controller Picks for 2026

1. Sony DualSense (Best for Most People)

The standard controller is still a masterpiece. Its adaptive triggers and haptic feedback provide immersion that third-party controllers often struggle to match.

  • Pros: Best haptics, built-in mic, most affordable.
  • Cons: Battery life is roughly 6-8 hours.

2. DualSense Edge (Best Pro Controller)

Sony's premium version features remappable back buttons, swappable stick modules (perfect for fixing drift), and adjustable triggers.

  • Pros: Premium build, highly customizable.
  • Cons: Significant price tag; lower battery life than the standard model.

3. Victrix Pro BFG (Best for Competitive Play)

A modular beast designed for FPS and Fighting games. You can swap the button layouts and it features ultra-fast wireless response times.

  • Pros: Hall Effect sticks, modular design, great battery.
  • Cons: No haptic feedback or adaptive triggers.

What to Look For

When buying a new pad in 2026, prioritize Back Paddles for shooters and Hall Effect Sticks for longevity.
