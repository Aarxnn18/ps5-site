Best PS5 Controllers (2026 Guide)

Whether you are looking for more immersion or a competitive edge, choosing the right controller is the biggest upgrade you can make to your PS5 setup.

Pro Tip: Look for controllers with Hall Effect sensors if you want to avoid "stick drift" permanently.

Top Controller Picks

1. Sony DualSense (Best for Most People)

The standard controller that comes with the console is still incredible thanks to its adaptive triggers and haptic feedback.

  • Pros: Best haptics, built-in microphone, affordable.
  • Cons: Battery life could be better.

2. DualSense Edge (Best Pro Controller)

Sony's high-end version features remappable back buttons, swappable stick modules, and adjustable trigger depths.

  • Pros: Premium build, highly customizable, replaceable parts.
  • Cons: Very expensive.

Common Questions (FAQ)

Can I use a PS4 controller on PS5?

Only for playing PS4 games. You must use a PS5-compatible controller to play native PS5 titles.

What is stick drift?

It's when your character or camera moves on its own. Newer 2026 models often use magnets (Hall Effect) to prevent this.
