How to Install an M.2 SSD

Tutorial | April 2026

Safety First

Ensure your PS5 is unplugged and has cooled down before opening the plates.

Installation Steps

  1. Remove the PS5 faceplate.
  2. Unscrew the expansion slot cover.
  3. Insert the SSD at a 45-degree angle.
  4. Secure the SSD with the screw and spacer.
