Managing "Cold Storage": Transferring PS5 Titles

Data Management | Updated April 2026

The Playability Restriction

You cannot play PS5-native games directly from a USB drive because the USB 3.2 interface is too slow for 2026 asset streaming. However, "Cold Storage" allows you to swap a 100GB game back and forth in minutes rather than re-downloading it. PS4 games, conversely, can be played directly from the external unit.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Rear Port Priority: Connect your external drive to one of the **USB-A SuperSpeed (10Gbps)** ports on the back of the console. The front port is for charging and is not recommended for mass storage.
  2. Formatting: Go to Settings > Storage > USB Extended Storage and select Format as USB Extended Storage. Warning: This wipes the drive.
  3. Mass Move: Navigate to Console Storage > Games and Apps. Select Move PS5 Games and tick the titles you wish to archive.
  4. The "Auto-Install" Rule: Set Always Install PS4 Games to USB in the storage settings to keep your internal SSD clear for high-performance PS5 Pro titles.
