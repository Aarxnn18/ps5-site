Best PS5 Streaming Gear (2026 Guide)

Sharing your gameplay has never been easier. Whether you want to stream directly from the console or build a professional PC-based setup, having the right gear is the difference between a blurry stream and a viral one.

Pro Tip: If you are serious about growth, use a Capture Card. It allows you to use professional overlays and alerts that you can't get from the built-in PS5 broadcast feature.

Top Gear Picks for 2026

1. Elgato Game Capture 4K X (Best Capture Card)

This is the gold standard for 2026. It supports 4K144 passthrough, meaning you can play in high resolution on your monitor while streaming to your audience at perfect quality.

2. Sony PS5 HD Camera

The easiest way to show your face. It plugs directly into the back of the console and features built-in background removal tools so you can "cut" yourself out of the frame.

3. Shure MV7+ (Best Microphone)

Audio is more important than video. This hybrid USB/XLR mic sounds professional and plugs directly into the PS5 or a PC setup.
