Best PS5 Streaming Gear: 2026 Creator's Guide

Content Creation | Updated May 2026

Expert Advice: The AV1 Revolution

In May 2026, streaming from a $899 PS5 Pro requires hardware that can keep up with its output. The Pro utilizes AV1 encoding, which provides 50% more visual detail at the same bitrate compared to older consoles. To capture this, an HDMI 2.1 capture card is non-negotiable to maintain your 4K/120Hz or 8K/60Hz visuals while you broadcast.
