PSVR2 Buying Guide (2026 Edition)

The PlayStation VR2 remains the most advanced consumer VR headset for console gamers. With its OLED HDR displays and innovative eye-tracking, it offers an immersion level that rivals expensive PC setups.

Pro Tip: Protect your investment! Never let direct sunlight hit the internal lenses, as it can permanently burn the OLED screen.

Must-Have Accessories for 2026

1. Globular Cluster CM2 Comfort Mod

The biggest complaint about PSVR2 is the forehead pressure. This comfort mod replaces the stock padding and adds a top strap, making long sessions much easier.

2. PSVR2 PC Adapter

Released by Sony to expand the headset's library, this adapter lets you plug your PSVR2 into a gaming PC to play thousands of SteamVR titles.
