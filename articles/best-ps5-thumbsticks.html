Controller Optimization | Updated April 2026

The Physics of Leverage

Performance thumbsticks work by increasing the "Arc Distance" of your analog stick. By raising the height of the right stick, you increase your range of motion by up to 120%, allowing for higher in-game sensitivity with finer aiming control. In 2026, specialized rubber compounds also provide superior moisture-wicking for long sessions.
