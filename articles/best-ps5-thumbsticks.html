Precision Gear: Best PS5 Performance Thumbsticks

Controller Optimization | Updated May 2026

The Physics of Leverage

Performance thumbsticks are the most cost-effective way to upgrade your $899 PS5 Pro experience. By increasing the "Arc Distance" of your analog stick, you raise your range of motion by up to 120%. This allows you to crank up in-game sensitivity for fast turns while maintaining the fine motor control needed for long-range precision. In 2026, we also prioritize modular kits that support the DualSense Edge's swappable stick system.
