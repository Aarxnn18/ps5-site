Best PS5 Headsets (2026 Guide)

To truly experience the PS5's Tempest 3D Audio, you need a headset that can deliver high-fidelity spatial sound.

Buyer's Advice: Check for dual-wireless support if you want to listen to game audio and your phone (Discord/Music) at the same time.

Top Audio Picks

1. SteelSeries Arctis Nova 5 (Best Overall)

A 2026 favorite that offers over 60 hours of battery life and a companion app with over 100 game-specific audio presets.

  • Pros: Massive battery, extremely comfortable, great mic.
  • Cons: Software can be complex for beginners.

2. PlayStation Pulse Elite (Best Integration)

Sony's premium headset uses planar magnetic drivers for studio-quality sound and includes a retractable noise-canceling mic.

  • Pros: Perfect PS5 integration, lossless audio, magnetic charging.
  • Cons: Unique design isn't for everyone.

What is Tempest 3D Audio?

It is Sony's custom audio engine that makes sound feel like it's coming from all directions. Any headset connected via USB or the 3.5mm jack can use it, but "Certified" headsets usually offer better tuning.

FAQ

Wireless vs Wired?

Wireless offers freedom, but wired headsets are often cheaper and never need charging. Both support 3D Audio on PS5.
