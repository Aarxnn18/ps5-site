Audio Engineering | Updated May 2026

Expert Advice: The High-Fidelity Gap

With the $899 PS5 Pro pushing more sound objects than ever before, the bottleneck in your audio is no longer the console—it's your drivers. In May 2026, we prioritize Planar Magnetic and Graphene drivers. These materials respond faster than traditional neodymium, allowing you to pinpoint footsteps in GTA VI or Marathon with surgical precision.
