Best PS5 Headsets (2026 Guide)

To truly experience the PS5's Tempest 3D Audio, you need a headset that can deliver high-fidelity spatial sound. In 2026, the market is filled with options that offer active noise cancellation and multi-device connectivity.

Buyer's Advice: Check for dual-wireless support if you want to listen to game audio and your phone at the same time.

Setup Guide: Just got a new pair? Follow our How to Enable & Optimize 3D Audio.

Top Audio Picks for 2026

1. SteelSeries Arctis Nova Pro Wireless (Best Overall)

The reigning champion of PS5 audio. It features an innovative dual-battery system so you never have to plug in a charging cable, alongside world-class Active Noise Cancellation (ANC).

  • Pros: Unlimited battery life (swappable), OLED base station, superb comfort.
  • Cons: Extremely expensive.

2. Sony Inzone H9 II (Best Official Alternative)

Sony's premium gaming line offers a sleek design that matches the PS5 perfectly. The H9 II improved on the original with better battery life and more breathable ear cushions.

  • Pros: Seamless PS5 integration, excellent spatial sound, great mic.
  • Cons: Bulky design compared to others.

3. PlayStation Pulse Elite (Best Integration)

Using planar magnetic drivers, the Pulse Elite offers studio-level audio detail at a mid-range price point. It’s the easiest way to experience 3D Audio as the developers intended.

  • Pros: Lossless audio, retractable mic, magnetic charging hanger.
  • Cons: The "look" is polarizing and the earcups aren't swappable.

What is Tempest 3D Audio?

Tempest 3D Audio is Sony's proprietary spatial audio technology. Unlike traditional surround sound, it uses hardware acceleration to treat every sound (like a footstep or a raindrop) as an individual object in space.
