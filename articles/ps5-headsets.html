3D Audio Masterclass: Best PS5 Headsets

Audio Engineering | Updated May 2026

Expert Advice: The High-Fidelity Gap

With the $899 PS5 Pro pushing more sound objects than ever before, the bottleneck in your audio is no longer the console—it's your drivers. In May 2026, we prioritize Planar Magnetic and Graphene drivers. These materials respond faster than traditional neodymium, allowing you to pinpoint footsteps in GTA VI or Marathon with surgical precision.

1. Audeze Maxwell - The Soundstage Master

The Audeze Maxwell remains the gold standard for PS5 Pro audio. Its massive 90mm Planar Magnetic drivers provide a level of separation and depth that makes standard headsets sound "flat." In our 2026 testing, this offered the most realistic 3D spatial imaging for open-world exploration.

2. SteelSeries Arctis Nova Elite - The Luxury Standard

The new "Elite" tier from SteelSeries adds higher-fidelity drivers and a premium build over the Pro model. It features a dedicated OLED base station for on-the-fly EQ mixing without ever leaving your game, perfectly matching the premium feel of a Pro-tier setup.

Check Nova Elite Price

3. Sony INZONE H6 Air - The Comfort Revolution

At a staggering 199g, the H6 Air is designed to disappear. Its open-back design prevents heat buildup and creates a naturally wide soundstage. It is our top recommendation for single-player gamers who prioritize long-term comfort during 100-hour RPG marathons.

4. Logitech G Pro X 2 Lightspeed - Precision Graphene

By using Graphene drivers, Logitech has achieved a level of clarity that is vital for competitive play. These drivers are virtually impossible to distort, ensuring that loud explosions won't drown out the subtle sound of an enemy reloading nearby.

Check Logitech Price

5. SteelSeries Arctis Nova Pro Wireless - The Endurance King

The Nova Pro remains on our list for one reason: the hot-swappable battery. In 2026, where wireless interference is at an all-time high, its robust 2.4GHz connection and "infinite battery" life make it the most reliable tool in a gamer's arsenal.
