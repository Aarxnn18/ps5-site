Best PS5 Headsets: 3D Audio immersion

Audiophile Review | April 2026

Understanding Tempest 3D Audio

Unlike standard surround sound, the PS5’s Tempest engine creates a sphere of sound. This allows you to hear the height and exact distance of a footstep. To take advantage of this, your headset needs a wide frequency response and high-quality drivers.

1. Sony Pulse Elite (Planar Magnetic Drivers)

The Pulse Elite is the first mainstream gaming headset to use Planar Magnetic drivers—technology usually reserved for $1,000 audiophile headphones. This results in near-zero distortion and lightning-fast transient response, making every gunshot and explosion crisp and clear.

  • Review: The soundstage is remarkably wide for a closed-back headset. The retractable mic features AI noise rejection that is best-in-class.

2. SteelSeries Arctis Nova Pro Wireless

This is the "Luxury Sedan" of gaming headsets. It features an OLED base station for hot-swapping batteries, meaning you never have to plug it into a charger. Its Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) is powerful enough to block out loud fans or AC units during gameplay.

  • Review: Extremely comfortable for 8-hour sessions. The dual-link wireless allows you to listen to Discord on your phone while playing on PS5.
