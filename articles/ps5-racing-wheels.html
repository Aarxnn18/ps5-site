Sim Racing Masterclass: Best PS5 Racing Wheels

Racing Gear | Updated April 2026

Expert Advice: Gear vs. Belt vs. Direct Drive

In 2026, the choice of motor determines your lap time. **Direct Drive (DD)** wheels are the gold standard, providing instantaneous force feedback. **Belt-driven** wheels offer a smooth, quiet middle ground, while **Gear-driven** wheels are the most affordable entry point for those starting their journey in Gran Turismo 7 or F1 26.
