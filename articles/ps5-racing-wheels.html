Racing Hardware | Updated May 2026

Expert Advice: The Direct Drive Standard

In 2026, the PS5 Pro's increased processing power has made high-frequency physics updates the norm. To truly feel the difference in tire grip and weight transfer, Direct Drive (DD) technology is now essential. Unlike older gear or belt systems, DD motors connect the steering wheel directly to the motor shaft, eliminating lag and mechanical "clunkiness" for a professional-grade simulation experience.

🏁 Sim Racing Hardware Checklist

Consider these factors before upgrading your cockpit:

  • Mounting: Direct Drive bases require a Rigid Cockpit; standard desks may flex under high torque.
  • Feedback Tech: Look for TRUEFORCE Integration to feel high-definition engine vibrations through the wheel.
  • Display Needs: Hybrid wheels with Integrated Dashboards help you track lap times without looking away from the screen.
  • Immersion: Pair your rig with 3D Audio Headsets to pinpoint surrounding traffic.
