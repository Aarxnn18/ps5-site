Racing Gear | Updated April 2026

Expert Advice: The Direct Drive Revolution

In 2026, the industry has shifted. While gear-driven wheels remain great for beginners, **Direct Drive (DD)** technology is now the benchmark for anyone serious about sim racing. It provides a level of detail and responsiveness that belt or gear systems simply cannot match, allowing you to feel every curb and loss of traction.
