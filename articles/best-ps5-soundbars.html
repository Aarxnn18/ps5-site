Best Dolby Atmos Soundbars for PS5 Pro

Audio Hardware | Updated May 2026

The Tempest-Atmos Handshake

In May 2026, the synergy between Sony's Tempest 3D Engine and Dolby Atmos has reached its peak. For owners of the $899 PS5 Pro, choosing a soundbar with HDMI 2.1 Passthrough is essential. This allows the bar to process high-fidelity audio while passing a 4K/120Hz signal with VRR directly to your display, ensuring zero input lag and maximum visual smoothness.

1. Samsung HW-Q990D - The Performance Leader

The Q990D remains the undisputed king for PS5 Pro users. It is one of the few systems that supports full HDMI 2.1 passthrough. With 11.1.4 channels, including dedicated wireless rears, it provides the most immersive bubble of sound available for GTA VI and other open-world titles.

2. Sony BRAVIA Theatre Bar 9 - The Official Partner

Designed specifically for the PS5 Pro era, the Bar 9 utilizes 360 Spatial Sound Mapping to create "phantom speakers" throughout your room. If you own a Bravia TV, it uses the screen as a center channel for unmatched dialogue clarity.

Check Sony Bar 9 Price

3. LG S95TR - The Dialogue Specialist

The S95TR features a world-first: a center up-firing speaker. This tech ensures that character voices aren't lost in the chaos of explosions and soundtracks, making it our top pick for narrative-driven RPGs.

Check LG S95TR Price

4. Sonos Arc Ultra - The Deep Bass King

The Arc Ultra's "Sound Motion" technology allows it to produce ground-shaking bass without a dedicated subwoofer. It’s the best "all-in-one" solution for users who want a clean setup but still want to feel every engine roar.

Check Sonos Price

5. Sennheiser AMBEO Soundbar Mini - Small Room Elite

Don't let the size fool you. The AMBEO Mini uses advanced spatial scaling to bounce sound off your walls, effectively tricking your ears into hearing a full 7.1.4 setup. Perfect for desk-bound PS5 Pro rigs.
