Best Dolby Atmos Soundbars for PS5 Pro

Audio Hardware | Updated April 2026

The Tempest-Atmos Handshake

Since the 2023/2024 firmware updates, the PS5 can render Tempest 3D Audio objects into a Dolby Atmos bitstream. This means your soundbar can now use its upward-firing drivers to create height channels for overhead sound effects, such as rain or aircraft, with pinpoint accuracy.
