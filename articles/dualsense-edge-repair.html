DualSense Edge: Replacing Stick Modules

Hardware Repair | 2026 Manual

The Modular Solution to Stick Drift

The DualSense Edge is designed for longevity. Unlike the standard controller, you do not need to solder new sensors to fix stick drift. You simply swap the entire mechanical housing. This modular design is the standard for pro-tier gaming in 2026.

Pro Tip: Before swapping the module, try cleaning the contact pins. If dust has entered the rail system, even a new module might disconnect. See our Best PS5 Toolkits for the precision cleaning tools required for pro-controller maintenance.

Step-by-Step Replacement

  1. Release the Cover: Slide the "Release" latch on the back of the controller to pop off the front black faceplate.
  2. Lift the Lever: Locate the small silver metal lever on the side of the stick module and pull it upward.
  3. Slide Out: Pull the old stick module toward you to disconnect it from the internal rails.
  4. Insert New Module: Slide the new module in until you feel it click, then push the silver lever back down to lock it.
  5. Customize: While the module is out, it is the perfect time to swap your stick caps. Check our Best Performance Thumbsticks for height-adjustable options compatible with the Edge.
