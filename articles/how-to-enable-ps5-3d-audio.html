How to Optimize Tempest 3D Audio

Optimization | Updated April 2026

Expert Guidance

Every ear shapes sound differently. Using the calibration tool is essential for accurately hearing footsteps in competitive games.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Sound Menu: Go to Settings > Sound.
  2. Select Output: Select 3D Audio (Headphones).
  3. Enable: Ensure Enable 3D Audio for Headphones is turned ON.
  4. Calibrate: Select Adjust 3D Audio Profile.
  5. Listen: Choose the profile where the sound matches your ear level most accurately.
