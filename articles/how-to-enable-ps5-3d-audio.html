How to Enable 3D Audio

Tutorial | April 2026

Pro Tip: 3D Audio now works for TV speakers as well as headsets, but headsets provide the most immersion.

Optimization Steps

  1. Go to Settings > Sound.
  2. Select 3D Audio (Headphones) or 3D Audio (TV Speakers).
  3. Switch on Enable 3D Audio.
  4. Select Measure Room Acoustics to calibrate for your specific environment.
