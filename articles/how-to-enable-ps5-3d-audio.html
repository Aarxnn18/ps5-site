How to Enable 3D Audio on PS5

What is 3D Audio? Sony's Tempest Engine simulates sound coming from all directions (above, below, and behind you), giving you a competitive edge in shooters.

Step 1: Connect Your Headset

Plug your headset into the DualSense controller's 3.5mm jack, or connect your wireless USB dongle to the console's front port.

Step 2: Access Sound Settings

From the PS5 home screen, go to Settings (the gear icon) > Sound > Audio Output.

Step 3: Enable 3D Audio

Scroll down to the "Headphones" section. Ensure the toggle for Enable 3D Audio for Headphones is turned ON.

Step 4: Personalize Your Profile

Everyone hears sound differently. Select Create Personalized 3D Audio Profile. The console will play sounds at different heights—choose the one that sounds most "level" with your ears to finish the setup.
