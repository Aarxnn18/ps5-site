How to Optimize Tempest 3D Audio

Optimization | Updated April 2026

Expert Guidance

Sony's Tempest Engine is designed to simulate hundreds of individual sound sources. However, because every ear shapes sound differently, the default profile often feels "flat." Using the calibration tool is essential for accurately tracking enemy footsteps in competitive titles.

Hardware Tip: While 3D Audio works via the controller jack, the built-in DAC often limits the dynamic range. For the full 2026 spatial experience, we recommend using a headset from our Best PS5 Headsets guide that supports PlayStation Link.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Sound Menu: Go to Settings > Sound.
  2. Select Output: Choose 3D Audio (Headphones). (Note: If using a soundbar, ensure you select 3D Audio for TV Speakers instead).
  3. Enable: Ensure Enable 3D Audio for Headphones is turned ON.
  4. Calibrate: Select Adjust 3D Audio Profile.
  5. Listen: Choose the profile (1-5) where the sound matches your ear level most accurately. If the sound feels like it's "above" you, try a lower profile.
