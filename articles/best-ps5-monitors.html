Best PS5 Monitors (2026 Guide)

To see what the PS5 is truly capable of, you need a monitor that supports HDMI 2.1. This allows for 4K resolution at a smooth 120 frames per second.

Technical Note: Ensure your monitor has HDMI 2.1. Older HDMI 2.0 monitors are usually capped at 60Hz when running in 4K.

Setup Help: Not getting 120Hz? Follow our 120Hz Enable Guide.

Top Monitor Picks for 2026

1. Gigabyte M28U (Best Budget 4K)

The M28U remains the best value for PS5 gamers. It offers a crisp 4K image and full HDMI 2.1 support without the "premium" price tag of luxury brands.

  • Specs: 28-inch, IPS Panel, 144Hz, HDMI 2.1.
  • Best For: Gamers who want 4K performance on a budget.

2. LG UltraGear OLED 27GS95QE (Best for Color)

If you want perfect blacks and near-instant response times, OLED is the way to go. This LG monitor makes HDR games look incredible.

  • Specs: 27-inch, OLED, 240Hz (PS5 capped at 120Hz), 0.03ms response.
  • Best For: Competitive shooters and cinematic adventures.
