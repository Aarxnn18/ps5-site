Best PS5 Pro Monitors: 4K 120Hz & OLED Displays

Display Technology | Updated May 2026

Expert Advice: The 480Hz Motion Leap

In May 2026, the $899 PS5 Pro has shifted the focus toward motion clarity. While the console caps at 120Hz, the new 480Hz OLED panels provide a near-instant pixel response that makes competitive shooters feel significantly more responsive. For cinematic games, PSSR upscaling ensures that 4K QD-OLED panels look sharper than ever before.

1. Sony INZONE M10S - The First-Party Elite

The M10S is the ultimate companion for the PS5 Pro. This 480Hz OLED monitor features "Auto HDR Tone Mapping," which automatically calibrates your console's HDR settings the moment it's plugged in. It offers the fastest motion response in the current first-party lineup.

2. ASUS ROG Swift OLED PG27AQDP - The Speed King

This is the world's first 480Hz WOLED monitor. Even at the PS5's 120Hz performance mode, the pixel transitions are so fast that motion blur is virtually non-existent. It’s our top pick for competitive Marathon or Call of Duty players.

Check ASUS OLED Price

3. Alienware AW3225QF - The Cinematic Standard

This 32-inch curved 4K QD-OLED remains a favorite for open-world games. Its 1700R curve provides an immersive field of vision, and the QD-OLED technology ensures colors pop with a vibrance that standard OLEDs can't match.

Check Alienware Price

4. Samsung Odyssey OLED G8 - Smart Visuals

The G80SD is a powerhouse of AI processing. It helps upscale sub-4K content to look native on its gorgeous QD-OLED panel. Its matte, glare-free coating makes it the best premium choice for gamers in bright rooms.

Check Samsung Price

5. LG UltraGear 32GS95UE - The Dual-Mode Hybrid

Perfect for those who split their time between PS5 and PC gaming. Its unique dual-mode allows you to toggle between 4K/240Hz for cinematic fidelity and a specialized 1080p/480Hz mode for ultra-competitive frame rates.
