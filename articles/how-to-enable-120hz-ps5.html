How to Enable 120Hz on Your PS5

Check Your Cable: You MUST use the Ultra High Speed HDMI cable that came with your PS5 (or a certified HDMI 2.1 cable) to reach 120Hz.

Step 1: Set Console to Performance Mode

Go to Settings > Saved Data and Game/App Settings > Game Presets. Change "Performance Mode or Resolution Mode" to Performance Mode.

Step 2: Enable 120Hz Output

Go to Settings > Screen and Video > Video Output. Ensure "Enable 120Hz Output" is set to Automatic.

Step 3: Test the Connection

In the same "Video Output" menu, scroll up to Information for the Connected HDMI Device. If it says "3840 x 2160 - 120Hz" under the supported frequencies, you are ready!
