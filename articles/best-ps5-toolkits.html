Essential Gear: Best Precision Toolkits for PS5

Tools & Equipment | Updated April 2026

Expert Advice: Why Bit Quality Matters

The PS5 uses specialized T8 Security Torx screws that are notoriously easy to strip. Using a "budget" screwdriver set made of soft steel can permanently damage your console's fasteners. We recommend kits using S2 Steel or higher for maximum durability and a perfect fit.
