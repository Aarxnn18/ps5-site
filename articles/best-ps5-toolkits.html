Expert Advice: The TR8 Security Standard

The $899 PS5 Pro uses specialized TR8 Security Torx screws to secure the fan housing and internal shielding. Unlike standard Torx bits, these have a small hole in the center to accommodate the security pin. Attempting to use a standard bit or soft-steel budget tools will likely strip these fasteners. In 2026, we exclusively recommend S2 Steel or higher to ensure a non-destructive repair experience.
