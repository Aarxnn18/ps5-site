Expert Guide: Best PS5 External Storage

Storage Hardware | Updated April 2026

Maximizing USB-C Data Rates

While internal M.2 SSDs are for active play, external drives allow you to store PS5 games or play PS4 titles directly from the drive. To avoid long loading screens, we recommend **Portable SSDs** that support at least **10Gbps** speeds. This ensures your game transfers take minutes, not hours.
