Expert Guide: Best PS5 External Storage

Storage Hardware | Updated May 2026

Cold Storage vs. Direct Play

With PS5 Pro Enhanced titles regularly exceeding 100GB, external storage is now a library management necessity. While you cannot play PS5 titles directly from a USB drive, you can use these SSDs as "Cold Storage" to swap games in minutes rather than re-downloading them. Additionally, all PS4 titles can be played directly from these drives, saving your precious internal M.2 space for high-performance current-gen games.

📦 External Storage Checklist

Optimize your cold storage efficiency with these technical requirements:

  • Port Selection: Always use the rear USB-A or USB-C ports; the front USB-A port is often restricted to charging and basic data.
  • File Management: Use Cold Storage for massive titles like GTA VI to avoid 200GB+ downloads.
  • Cable Quality: Ensure you use the provided SuperSpeed USB (10Gbps) cable; standard phone charging cables will limit transfer speeds significantly.
  • Compatibility: Drives must be at least 250GB and no more than 8TB to be recognized as Extended Storage.
