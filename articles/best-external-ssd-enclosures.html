Expert Guide: Best External NVMe Enclosures

Storage Hardware | Updated April 2026

Maximizing USB-C Data Rates

While internal M.2 SSDs are for active play, external NVMe enclosures allow you to store PS5 games at a fraction of the cost. To avoid bottlenecks, you must use an enclosure that supports **USB 3.2 Gen 2 (10Gbps)**. Cheaper 5Gbps enclosures will double your transfer times, wasting the speed of your NVMe drive.
