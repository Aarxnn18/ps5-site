How to Pair Your PS5 Controller

Quick Fix: If your controller won't connect, try using a high-quality USB-C cable directly to the front port of the PS5.

1. Pairing to a PS5 Console

The easiest way to sync is to use the USB-C cable that came with your console. Plug it in, press the PS Button, and your controller will pair instantly.

2. Pairing via Bluetooth (PC or Mobile)

To put the DualSense into "Pairing Mode," press and hold the Create Button (left of the touchpad) and the PS Button at the same time until the light bar starts flashing rapidly.

Once it's flashing, find "Wireless Controller" in your device's Bluetooth settings and select it.

3. How to Reset a Glitchy Controller

If you're experiencing lag or "drift," locate the small reset hole on the back of the controller. Use a paperclip to press the button inside for 5 seconds while the console is off.
